ABOUT US


CHRC Design and Build a dream come true by Vanshaj Raj Bahal in June 2019 with an aim to challenge the construction Industry and manage it in such a way in which each and every project is to be handed over with an assured and guaranteed time, cost and scope, as promised by us. We are dedicated to fresh perspectives with experienced supervision and our passion lies in completing projects step by step from Design Development to Project Management and to the final handing over with Execution of work at site as per the requirements of client in Desired Time, Cost & Scope while maintaining the best Quality of it with the help of processes based on PMBOK. Leave the building to the professionals!


BEHIND CHRC


CHRC Design & Build is a firm founded by Vanshaj Raj Bahal, with an aim to redefine his dad’s 20 year old dream project dedicated to his mother, in continuation he started this firm dedicated to his Parents. Vanshaj has a bachelor degree in Civil Engineering & post graduate in Construction & Real Estate Management and have hands on experience of working with L&T and India’s growing PMC Manomav Engineers
